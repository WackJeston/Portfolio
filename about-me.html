About Me

My name is Jack Weston, I am 21 years old and I am currently on the Scion Coalition Scheme. I have always had a love for everything computers and dreamed of learning how to code, but I thought it was beyond myself. The SCS has proved me wrong by showing me just how easy it is to learn. I am only two months into the course and I have already learnt 2 languages and made multiple websites.

Before finding the SCS course I worked as a graphic designer for a couple of years, and whilst doing this I got the chance to use platforms like Wix, Squarespace, and Wordpress which started my desire to design websites.

I aim to learn all aspects of web development from front to back, but I do want to specialize in front-end development. I believe that my strong knowledge of the Adobe suite and past experience designing will help me achieve this.
